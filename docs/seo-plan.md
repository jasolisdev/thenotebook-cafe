# The Notebook Cafe SEO Plan

## Summary of What We Know
- Business: The Notebook Cafe, a coffee shop in Riverside, CA.
- Audience: Local community (broad, "everyone" locally).
- Goals: More foot traffic, stronger Google Maps visibility, higher rankings.
- Priority items: Iced Brown Sugar Oat, Matcha Cloud, Classic Cold Brew, Espresso Tonic.

## Proposed On-Page Copy Suggestions

### Homepage
- Title: "The Notebook Cafe | Coffee Shop in Riverside, CA"
- Meta description: "Specialty coffee and community vibes in Riverside, CA. Visit The Notebook Cafe for espresso, matcha, cold brew, and signature pours."
- H1 enhancement (add a line near the main headline): "The Notebook Cafe — Riverside, CA"
- Signature pours paragraph (place near featured items):
  "Discover our signature pours in Riverside, CA: the Iced Brown Sugar Oat, Matcha Cloud, Classic Cold Brew, and Espresso Tonic. Crafted daily for a smooth, memorable cup."

### Menu Page
- Title: "Menu | The Notebook Cafe, Riverside CA"
- Meta description: "Explore The Notebook Cafe menu in Riverside, CA. Espresso, cold brew, matcha, and seasonal favorites crafted daily."
- Hero subtitle: "Handcrafted Daily in Riverside, CA"
- Add static menu text (even if full drinks menu is coming soon):
  "Signature drinks include the Iced Brown Sugar Oat, Matcha Cloud, Classic Cold Brew, and Espresso Tonic. Ask about seasonal specials and featured roasts."

### Story Page
- Title: "Our Story | The Notebook Cafe, Riverside CA"
- Meta description: "Learn how The Notebook Cafe became Riverside's community hub for specialty coffee, creativity, and curated sound."
- Hero subtitle: "Built by Riverside locals, for Riverside."

### Contact Page
- Title: "Contact | The Notebook Cafe, Riverside CA"
- Meta description: "Visit The Notebook Cafe in Riverside, CA. Find hours, location, and contact information."
- Add a short intro line above the address block:
  "Visit our Riverside cafe for specialty coffee and a welcoming space to work or relax."
